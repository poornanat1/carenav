# CareNav Gold — Summary of Benefits and Coverage

This Summary of Benefits and Coverage (SBC) is a synthetic example built for the CareNav
demo, formatted to mirror the federal SBC template. The numbers correspond to the
CareNav Gold plan in the demo dataset.

## Overall deductible

The overall plan deductible is $1,000 per year for an individual. You must generally pay
the costs of covered services up to the deductible before the plan begins to pay its
share. Some preventive services are covered at no cost and are not subject to the
deductible.

## Out-of-pocket limit

The out-of-pocket limit for this plan is $4,000 per year for an individual. This is the
most you could pay during the plan year for covered services. Once you reach it, the
plan pays 100% of covered benefits for the remainder of the year. Premiums and balance
billing from out-of-network providers do not count toward this limit.

## Common medical services

A primary care visit has a low copay with no deductible. A specialist visit has a modest
copay. Diagnostic imaging such as an MRI is covered at coinsurance after the deductible
and requires prior authorization. Emergency room visits are covered, and the
emergency-room copay is waived if you are admitted to the hospital.

## Prescription drugs

This plan covers generic, preferred brand, and specialty drugs, with generics at the
lowest cost share. Certain high-cost drugs require prior authorization. Mail-order
pharmacy may lower cost for maintenance medicines. The formulary lists which drugs are
covered and at what tier.

## Excluded and limited services

Generally excluded services include cosmetic procedures, long-term custodial care, and
adult routine dental and vision exams beyond what the plan specifies. Habilitation and
rehabilitation services may be limited per year. Refer to the full policy for the
complete list of covered and excluded services.
