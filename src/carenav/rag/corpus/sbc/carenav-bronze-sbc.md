# CareNav Bronze — Summary of Benefits and Coverage

This Summary of Benefits and Coverage (SBC) is a synthetic example built for the CareNav
demo, formatted to mirror the federal SBC template. The numbers correspond to the
CareNav Bronze plan in the demo dataset. Bronze is the lowest-premium tier, so it carries
a higher deductible and more coinsurance than the Silver or Gold plans.

## Overall deductible

The overall plan deductible is $4,000 per year for an individual. You must generally pay
the costs of covered services up to the deductible before the plan begins to pay its
share. Some preventive services are covered at no cost and are not subject to the
deductible.

## Out-of-pocket limit

The out-of-pocket limit for this plan is $8,000 per year for an individual. This is the
most you could pay during the plan year for covered services. Once you reach it, the
plan pays 100% of covered benefits for the remainder of the year. Premiums and balance
billing from out-of-network providers do not count toward this limit.

## Common medical services

A primary care office visit has a $40 copay. A specialist visit has a $75 copay.
Preventive care is covered at 100% with no cost share. Diagnostic imaging such as an MRI
is covered at 40% coinsurance after the deductible and requires prior authorization.
Diagnostic lab panels are covered at 40% coinsurance. Emergency room visits have a $500
copay, which is waived if you are admitted to the hospital.

## Prescription drugs

This plan covers generic, preferred brand, and specialty drugs, with generics at the
lowest cost share. Because Bronze uses coinsurance broadly, brand and specialty drugs can
carry a higher share of cost than on richer plans. Certain high-cost drugs require prior
authorization. Mail-order pharmacy may lower cost for maintenance medicines. The
formulary lists which drugs are covered and at what tier.

## Excluded and limited services

Generally excluded services include cosmetic procedures, long-term custodial care, and
adult routine dental and vision exams beyond what the plan specifies. Habilitation and
rehabilitation services may be limited per year. Durable medical equipment and devices
not listed in the plan's covered-benefits schedule are not covered. Refer to the full
policy for the complete list of covered and excluded services.
